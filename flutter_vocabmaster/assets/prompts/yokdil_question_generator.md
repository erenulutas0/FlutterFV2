## BÖLÜM 1: GRAMMAR (Dilbilgisi) - 14 Soru

### Format:
- 1-2 boşluklu cümleler
- 5 şık (A-E)
- Akademik/bilimsel konular

### Alt Kategoriler:

#### 1.1 Tense + Voice (Zaman + Çatı)
```
Örnek: A theory ---- only when a hypothesis ---- by consistent results from many observations.
A) may have been developed / was being supported
B) can be developed / has been supported ✓
C) will be developed / was supported
D) has been developed / had been supported
E) had been developed / might have been supported
```

**Üretim Kuralları:**
- İki boşluk olsun
- Birinci boşluk: Active voice
- İkinci boşluk: Passive voice
- Tense kombinasyonları: Present Perfect + Past Simple, Modal + Present Perfect, Past Perfect + Past Simple
- Yanlış şıklar: Tense uyumsuzluğu, voice hatası

#### 1.2 Conjunctions (Bağlaçlar)
```
Örnek: ---- astronomers have so far found no Earth-like planets, this does not mean that there are none.
A) While
B) As
C) Even though ✓
D) As if
E) Unless
```

**Üretim Kuralları:**
- Contrast: Although, Even though, While, Whereas
- Cause: Since, Because, As
- Condition: If, Unless, Provided that
- Concession: Despite, In spite of
- Yanlış şıklar: Anlam uyumsuzluğu

#### 1.3 Correlative Conjunctions (İkili Bağlaçlar)
```
Örnek: Products ---- diverse ---- rubber, tobacco, and coffee come from flowering plants.
A) as / as ✓
B) too / than
C) both / and
D) either / or
E) not only / but also
```

**Üretim Kuralları:**
- as...as, so...that, neither...nor, either...or, both...and, not only...but also
- Yapı uyumu kritik

## BÖLÜM 2: VOCABULARY (Kelime Bilgisi) - 6 Soru

### 2.1 Phrasal Verbs (1 soru)
```
Örnek: The moon maps are incomplete but it is hoped that the 2008 lunar orbiter will ---- the gaps for us.
A) make up
B) fill in ✓
C) lay out
D) put over
E) work out
```

**Sık Çıkan Phrasal Verbs:**
- fill in, come up with, point out, account for, keep pace with
- wipe out, die out, figure out, lead to, build up
- work out, make up of, break out, turn up, take over

### 2.2 Academic Vocabulary (5 soru)
```
Örnek: His efforts to ---- the threat of global warming with new forms of energy have been much appreciated.
A) excuse
B) counter ✓
C) pursue
D) uphold
E) deliver
```

**Kelime Kategorileri:**
- Verbs: counter, exert, speculate, abandon, release, discredit
- Adjectives: comprehensive, coherent, proportional, inevitable, prevalent
- Nouns: distinction, exposure, disturbance, gap, objection
- Adverbs: continuously, increasingly, considerably, necessarily

## BÖLÜM 3: PREPOSITIONS (Edatlar) - 6 Soru

### Format:
```
Örnek: The most useful geothermal resources are hot water with temperatures ranging ---- 80° ---- 350° centigrade.
A) from / to ✓
B) between / of
C) among / at
D) within / over
E) under / off
```

**Sık Çıkan Kalıplar:**
- Collocation: worry about, familiar with, capable of, depend on
- Range: from X to Y, between X and Y
- Time: during, throughout, within, for
- Location: in, at, on, above, under
- Agent: by + person, through + method

## BÖLÜM 4: CLOZE TEST - 10 Soru (2 paragraf x 5 soru)

### Format:
Bir paragraf verilir, 5 numaralı boşluk var. Her boşluk için 5 şık.

### Paragraf Örneği:
```
Japan is home to almost half the world's industrial robots. Only a few decades ago, when robots (1)---- for the first time, they were used for less sophisticated tasks, (2)---- humans were considered essential for manual precision tasks. This, however, changed with (3)---- in robot technology. Today, robots are used in dirty, dangerous, and inaccessible tasks as well as in (4)---- requiring precision. Today, robots are an indispensable part (5)---- industrial Japan.
```

### Boşluk Tipleri:
1. **Tense/Voice**: were introduced, had been introduced, have been introduced
2. **Conjunctions**: while, whereas, so that, in case
3. **Nouns**: advances, devices, conclusions
4. **Pronouns/Determiners**: those, which, whose
5. **Prepositions**: of, in, for, at

## BÖLÜM 5: SENTENCE COMPLETION - 11 Soru

### Format:
Yarım cümle verilir, tamamlayan şık seçilir.

### Örnek:
```
Although rain forests occupy a very small percentage of the land surface on Earth, ----.
A) it is true that mild winters are good for them
B) most governments have introduced severe measures to stop environmental pollution
C) they contain a very large variety of plant and animal species that are mostly rare ✓
D) many species living in those areas are faced with the threat of extinction
E) the Brazilian government has long-term plans to open up the Amazon basin
```

### Bağlaç Sinyalleri:
- **Although/Even though** → Zıt sonuç bekle
- **If/Unless** → Koşul-sonuç
- **Since/Because** → Neden açıklaması
- **----, which** → Açıklama/detay
- **----, but** → Zıtlık

## BÖLÜM 6: TRANSLATION - 12 Soru

### 6.1 İngilizce → Türkçe (6 soru)
```
English: Chemistry, as a field of study based on scientific principles, came into being in the latter part of the eighteenth century.

A) Bilimsel ilkelere dayalı bir çalışma alanı olarak kimya, on sekizinci yüzyılın son bölümünde ortaya çıkmıştır. ✓
B) Çok öncelerden beri var olan kimya, on sekizinci yüzyılın son bölümünde bilimsel ilkelere dayalı bir çalışma alanı olarak gelişmiştir.
C) On sekizinci yüzyılın son bölümüne doğru ortaya çıkan kimya, bilimsel ilkelere dayalı bir çalışma alanıdır.
D) Bilimsel ilkelere dayalı bir çalışma alanı olan kimya, on sekizinci yüzyılın son bölümünde gelişmesini tamamlamıştır.
E) On sekizinci yüzyılın son bölümünde ortaya çıkan bilimsel ilkelere dayalı çalışma alanlarından biri kimyadır.
```

### Yanlış Şık Oluşturma:
- **Anlam kaydırması**: "ortaya çıkmak" → "gelişmek"
- **Zaman hatası**: Simple Past → Present Perfect
- **Edilgen/Etken karışıklık**
- **Kelime ekleme/çıkarma**

### 6.2 Türkçe → İngilizce (6 soru)
Aynı format, ters yönde.

## BÖLÜM 7: PARAGRAPH COMPLETION - 6 Soru

### Format:
4-5 cümlelik paragraf, ortada veya sonda boşluk.

### Örnek:
```
Otto Lehmann observed that liquid crystals are remarkably sensitive. ---- Further, they can register the minute fluctuations in temperature by a change in colour.

A) Liquid crystals in thermometers, thermographs, computers, TVs were too far in the future for him to imagine.
B) Lehmann dedicated 25 years of work to studying these strange chemicals.
C) In his last book he suggested many applications, mostly in power generation.
D) They respond to heat, light, sound, mechanical pressure, electromagnetic fields and radiation and even some chemical vapours. ✓
E) Many of them wrote off liquid crystals as chemical impurities with no scientific merit.
```

### Bağlantı Sinyalleri:
- **Further** → Ek bilgi bekle
- **However** → Zıtlık bekle
- **For instance** → Örnek bekle
- **As such** → Sonuç bekle

## BÖLÜM 8: IRRELEVANT SENTENCE - 6 Soru

### Format:
5 numaralı cümle (I-V), konu dışı olanı bul.

### Örnek:
```
(I) The atmosphere has three prevailing winds that blow more or less continually.
(II) The solar energy that reaches polar regions is less concentrated and produces lower temperatures.
(III) Prevailing winds that blow from the northeast near the North Pole are called "polar easterlies."
(IV) Winds that blow in the mid-latitudes from the southwest are "westerlies".
(V) Tropical winds are referred to as "trade winds."

A) I    B) II ✓    C) III    D) IV    E) V
```

### Konu Dışı Tespit:
- Farklı konu (topic shift)
- Farklı zaman dilimi
- Farklı coğrafya
- İlgisiz istatistik

## BÖLÜM 9: READING PASSAGES - 15 Soru (5 paragraf x 3 soru)

### Format:
Uzun paragraf (8-12 cümle), 3 soru.

### Soru Kalıpları:
```
• "According to the passage, ..."
• "We understand from the passage that ..."
• "It is clear from the passage that ..."
• "One point made in the passage is that ..."
• "It is pointed out in the passage that ..."
• "The point is made in the passage that, although ..."
```

### Soru Tipleri:
1. **Detay Sorusu**: Paragraftaki spesifik bilgi
2. **Çıkarım Sorusu**: Paragraftan ne anlıyoruz?
3. **Ana Fikir**: Paragrafın genel mesajı

### Örnek Paragraf:
```
A curious form of renewable-energy generation may be on the horizon – with underwater turbines. These units harness the power of tidally driven currents, which flow back and forth like clockwork, making it possible to generate electricity on a predictable schedule. In this respect, underwater turbines are more attractive than their wind-driven counterparts, which are now employed widely to help power electric grids. Underwater structures are also less likely to be damaged by violent storms, which have little effect on submerged objects. And using underwater turbines sidesteps the common objections to conventional hydropower – that damming a river stops migrating fish and inundates land upstream. Underwater turbines have long been used on a small scale. But soon now they will be used on a large scale to produce megawatts of electric power.
```

### Örnek Sorular:
```
1. We understand from the passage that underwater turbines ----.
A) disrupt the migration of fish in coastal water
B) are not as efficient as wind-driven ones
C) have for many years been the main means of obtaining renewable energy
D) are not dependent on tidal currents in order to function
E) existed in the past but were not widely used ✓

2. It is pointed out in the passage that one advantage of harnessing the tidal flow to generate electricity is that ----.
A) it is far more cost-effective than conventional hydropower
B) it has a positive effect on the environment
C) we know how much power can be produced at any given time ✓
D) there is little in the system that can go wrong except for the turbines
E) the same design of underwater turbines can be used in all seas

3. According to the passage, work is underway to ----.
A) prevent the inundation of land resulting from dams
B) make more use of underwater turbines to generate electricity ✓
C) step up the efficiency of underwater turbines
D) identify various sources of renewable energy
E) enlarge and renovate existing electric grids
```

## YANILTICI ŞIK (DISTRACTOR) OLUŞTURMA KURALLARI

### 1. Kısmi Doğruluk
Şık kısmen doğru ama kritik bir detay yanlış.

### 2. Aşırı Genelleme
Paragrafta belirtilenden daha geniş bir iddia.

### 3. Tersine Çevirme
Paragrafın söylediğinin tam tersini söyleme.

### 4. Konu Kayması
Aynı alan ama farklı odak noktası.

### 5. Paragrafta Olmayan Bilgi
Mantıklı görünüyor ama paragrafta yok.

### 6. Zaman/Nicelik Hatası
"always", "never", "all", "none" gibi mutlak ifadeler.
